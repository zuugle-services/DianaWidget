Your Day Trip to Stuhleck

Experience the thrill of the slopes at Stuhleck am Semmering, one of Austria's most popular and accessible ski resorts near Vienna. With perfectly groomed pistes catering to all skill levels, from gentle beginner slopes to challenging runs for experts, Stuhleck offers a fantastic winter sports experience. Enjoy the modern lifts, breathtaking panoramic views, and cozy mountain huts serving delicious Austrian specialties.

Forget the hassle of driving and finding parking! Use our integrated travel planner to find convenient train and bus connections directly to the resort. Simply enter your starting location and preferred date to discover the best public transport options for your unforgettable ski day.

Resort Highlights

  • Modern Lifts & Gondolas
  • 26 km of Diverse Pistes
  • Night Skiing Available
  • Cozy Mountain Restaurants

Recommended Gear

  • Warm, waterproof ski jacket and pants
  • Thermal base layers
  • Ski gloves or mittens
  • Helmet and goggles
  • Sunscreen and lip balm (even on cloudy days!)
  • Skis/Snowboard, boots, and poles (rentals available)
  • Backpack for water and snacks

Your Adventure to the Ice Kingdom

Embark on an unforgettable journey into the Marktschellenberger Eishöhle, Germany's largest ice cave, nestled within the majestic Untersberg massif. Discover a subterranean world of glittering ice formations, frozen waterfalls, and vast caverns. This natural wonder offers a unique experience for adventurers and nature lovers alike, showcasing the raw beauty sculpted by millennia of ice and rock.

Accessing the ice cave is an adventure in itself, typically involving a scenic hike. Our travel planner helps you find the best public transport connections to the starting points of the trails leading to this icy marvel. Prepare for a cool experience, both literally and figuratively!

Cave Highlights

  • Spectacular Ice Formations
  • Germany's Largest Ice Cave
  • Guided Tours Available
  • Consistently Low Temperatures (around 0°C)

Recommended Gear

  • Warm, layered clothing (temperature inside is near freezing)
  • Waterproof and sturdy hiking boots with good grip
  • Gloves and a warm hat
  • Headlamp or flashlight (though tours are usually lit)
  • Small backpack for water and snacks
  • Sunscreen and sunglasses for the hike to the cave
  • Camera to capture the icy beauty!

Your Adventure to the Ice Kingdom

Embark on an unforgettable journey into the Marktschellenberger Eishöhle, Germany's largest ice cave, nestled within the majestic Untersberg massif. Discover a subterranean world of glittering ice formations, frozen waterfalls, and vast caverns. This natural wonder offers a unique experience for adventurers and nature lovers alike, showcasing the raw beauty sculpted by millennia of ice and rock.

Accessing the ice cave is an adventure in itself, typically involving a scenic hike. Our travel planner helps you find the best public transport connections to the starting points of the trails leading to this icy marvel. Prepare for a cool experience, both literally and figuratively!

Cave Highlights

  • Spectacular Ice Formations
  • Germany's Largest Ice Cave
  • Guided Tours Available
  • Consistently Low Temperatures (around 0°C)

Recommended Gear

  • Warm, layered clothing (temperature inside is near freezing)
  • Waterproof and sturdy hiking boots with good grip
  • Gloves and a warm hat
  • Headlamp or flashlight (though tours are usually lit)
  • Small backpack for water and snacks
  • Sunscreen and sunglasses for the hike to the cave
  • Camera to capture the icy beauty!

Conquer the Rax: A 2-Day Alpine Adventure

Experience the breathtaking beauty of the Rax massif on an invigorating 2-day hike. This adventure is perfect for those looking to immerse themselves in alpine scenery, enjoy stunning vistas, and spend a night in a cozy mountain hut. The Rax offers a variety of trails, from gentle paths across alpine meadows to more challenging ascents.

Our planned route takes you through some of the most scenic parts of the Rax, with an overnight stay at a traditional Austrian "Hütte". Wake up to crisp mountain air and unparalleled views before continuing your journey. This trip is designed to be a memorable escape into nature.

Rax Hike Highlights

  • Stunning Alpine Scenery & Panoramas
  • Overnight Stay in a Mountain Hut
  • Varied Trails for Different Fitness Levels
  • Fresh Mountain Air & Tranquility

Recommended Gear for 2-Day Hike

  • Backpack (30-40 liters)
  • Sturdy, broken-in hiking boots
  • Weather-appropriate clothing (layers, rain gear, warm jacket)
  • Sleeping bag liner (for hut stays)
  • Headlamp or flashlight
  • Water bottles or hydration system (min. 2 liters)
  • High-energy snacks and food for 2 days
  • First-aid kit
  • Sunscreen, sunglasses, and sun hat
  • Map and compass/GPS device
  • Cash (for hut and potential transport)
  • Toiletries and personal medication
